# ECE R42 バンパー基準

## Article 1 Scope
This Regulation applies to vehicles of category M1 with regard to the behaviour of their front and rear protective devices when involved in a collision at low speed.

## Article 2 Definitions
2.1 "Protective device" means the section of the vehicle, within the meaning of paragraph 2.2. below, which is intended to protect the vehicle against the effects of a collision.

## 第3条 試験条件
3.1 試験車両は、無積載状態であること。
3.2 タイヤ空気圧は、メーカー推奨値とする。
